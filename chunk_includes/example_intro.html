WELCOME!

You are invited to participate in a research study on language. The purpose of this study is to investigate some of the properties of natural language.

What we will ask you to do:
If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to read sentences and rate them according to their naturalness, and/or answers questions about their content. Before beginning the study, you will be asked to fill out some basic information about your linguistic background and demographics.

Risks and benefits:
This experiment involves no known risks or discomfort to you, nor is it expected to be of any direct benefit to you. You will receive a compensation for your participation through Amazon Mechanical Turk. Be sure to enter the correct worker ID at the end of the experiment so we can process your payment.

Taking part is voluntary:
Your participation is completely voluntary. You are free to withdraw consent and discontinue participation in the project at any time.

Your answers will be confidential:
The responses will be stored on a password protected computer. The results we collect will not be linked to your identity.
